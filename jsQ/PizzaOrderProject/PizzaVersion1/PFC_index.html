Meet The Staff!

Delivery

Making sure your pizza arrives on time is what Boots does best. Rated the city's fastest delivery service three years in a row! Boots is a favorite of the ladies too - being known to include flowers with his orders.

Server

Service with a smile is what you'll find here - and Tom is one of our best! He started in the kitchen so he knows what to recommend for every finicky eater.

Head Chef

Tiger has won many awards for her specialty crusts. She started with Pizza For Cats at an early age and is considered a prodigy amongst chefs. Under her sweet face lies a cooking monster.

Procurement

Shadow knows how to get the job done - and makes sure there is always great in-season variety for our toppings! She started life on the streets and is well-equipped to obtain some of our more exotic ingredients.

Counter Clerk

Another smiling face at Pizza For Cats, Princess is one of our newest members, and favorite of the guys due to her piercing green eyes.

Security

Fluffy does NOT let the dogs out. He comes from a long line of security experts. Don't let his young age fool you - he sees everything - and you won't even feel the razor thin nails until it's too late..

Operations

Order Clerk

Ghost loves to extend her claws and get to work scratching out your order quickly and error free! She's one of the many friendly faces ready to help you at Pizza For Cats!

Payroll

Boots is under a lot of pressure as the store continues to grow and add more workers. Finding great staff at a fair pay keeps him crunching a lot of numbers. As a personal friend of the Founder, he also handles the company accounting.

Web Master

The Ovenator

Ice runs through Kitty-Girl's veins; it has to because she makes sure the ovens bake the pizzas at just the right temperature. It's her mission to keep all Pizza For Cats items the perfect temperature at delivery time.

Founder

Senge Alexander Sterling has refuted all claims he is involved in the mafia - let alone being a Dom. His vision for Pizza For Cats is focused and product-driven. Also known for his insistence that everything to be aesthetically pleasing.
